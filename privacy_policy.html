Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

We take your privacy very seriously. PillTime is a local application, and the information we collect includes:

  • Medication information you enter (medication name, dose, medication time, etc.)
  • Medication records and reminder settings
  • Application preference settings (such as Elder Mode, reminder count, etc.)
  • Medication photos (optional): Used to identify medication names and record medication appearance

All data is stored locally on your device, and we do not upload your personal information to any server.

Subscription Information

  • Subscription features are processed through Apple App Store
  • We do not collect, store, or process your payment information
  • Subscription status and transaction information are stored locally on your device
  • Subscription information is only used to unlock application features

Image Information

  • Scanned or photographed medication images are only used to identify medication names
  • Images are stored in the application sandbox and are not saved to the system photo album
  • Images are not uploaded to servers or shared with third parties
  • You can delete medication photos at any time

Data Security Storage

All your data is securely stored on your device:

  • Sensitive data is protected using AES-GCM encryption algorithm
  • Encryption keys are stored in iOS Keychain with device-level security protection
  • Each encryption uses a unique random number (Nonce) to ensure data security
  • Data includes version numbers and checksums to ensure data integrity

iCloud Backup

  • All user data has been configured to exclude iCloud backup
  • Your medication data will not be automatically backed up to iCloud
  • This ensures your private data will not be accidentally synced to other devices
  • If you need to backup data, please use the export function within the application

We do not access, collect, or transmit any of your personal health information.

Data Usage Purposes

PillTime uses your data only for:

  • Providing medication reminder features
  • Generating medication records and statistics
  • Improving application experience

Data Analysis

  • We record application usage to improve user experience
  • Currently, all analysis data is stored only locally on your device
  • We do not upload analysis data to servers
  • If we integrate third-party analysis services in the future, we will update this privacy policy and notify you

We will not use your data for any other purpose, nor will we share it with third parties.

Security Measures

We take the following measures to protect your data security:

  • Local encrypted storage (AES-GCM algorithm)
  • Encryption keys stored in iOS Keychain
  • No network transmission
  • No collection of user identity information
  • Sensitive data excluded from iCloud backup

Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Cross-App and Cross-Site Tracking

  • This app does not perform cross-app or cross-site tracking
  • We will not request tracking permissions using Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework
  • We do not share your data with third parties for advertising tracking purposes
  • The app does not display ads and does not collect data for ad delivery

Advertising Services

  • This app does not contain any advertisements
  • We do not use any third-party advertising services (such as AdMob, Google Mobile Ads, etc.)
  • We do not collect data for advertising delivery or personalized advertising
  • The app does not display promotional content or third-party advertisements

Third-Party Services

Apple StoreKit:

  • We use Apple StoreKit to process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Subscription-related transaction information is processed by Apple
  • We do not access your Apple ID or payment information
  • For how Apple handles your data, see Apple's Privacy Policy

Other Third-Party Services:

  • Currently, no other third-party services are used
  • If we integrate third-party services in the future, we will update this policy and notify you

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete all data within the app at any time
  • Export your medication records (JSON format)
  • Stop using the application at any time
  • Manage notification and calendar permissions in system settings
  • Cancel subscriptions (through App Store settings)

Data Deletion

  • Deleting the app clears all locally stored data including: medication info, records, reminder settings, preferences
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered. Please proceed with caution
  • Subscription info is managed by Apple. Deleting the app does not affect subscription status

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us via:

  • In-app feedback function
  • Email: chenyisy@gmail.com

We will respond to your inquiries promptly.

Legal Jurisdiction

This privacy policy is governed by the laws of the People's Republic of China. Disputes shall be resolved through friendly consultation; if consultation fails, either party may file a lawsuit with a people's court with jurisdiction.
